# Al-Humazah
## The Slanderer

In the Name of God, the All-Merciful, the Most Merciful.

1. Woe unto every slanderer, backbiter,
2. who gathereth wealth and counteth it,
3. thinking that his wealth hath made him immortal.
4. Nay! He shall surely be cast into the Crusher.
5. And what shall make thee know what the Crusher is?
6. The kindled fire of God,
7. which leapeth up over the hearts.
8. Verily, it is closed upon them
9. in pillars outstretched.
