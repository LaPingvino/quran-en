# Al-Masad
## The Fibre

In the Name of God, the All-Merciful, the Most Merciful.

1. Perish the hands of Abu Lahab, and perish he!
2. His wealth availed him not, nor what he earned.
3. He shall enter a fire of flame
4. and his wife, the carrier of firewood,
5. around her neck a rope of palm-fibre.
