# Al-Kafirun
## Those Who Cover Over

In the Name of God, the All-Merciful, the Most Merciful.

1. Say: O ye who conceal!
2. I worship not what ye worship
3. and ye worship not what I worship.
4. And I shall not worship what ye have worshipped
5. and ye will not worship what I worship.
6. Unto you your religion, and unto me my religion.
