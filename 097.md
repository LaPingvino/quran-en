# Al-Qadr
## The Power

In the Name of God, the All-Merciful, the Most Merciful.

1. Verily, We sent it down in the Night of Power.
2. And what shall make thee know what the Night of Power is?
3. The Night of Power is better than a thousand months.
4. The angels and the Spirit descend therein, by the leave of their Lord, with every command.
5. Peace it is, until the rising of the dawn.
