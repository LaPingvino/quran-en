# Az-Zalzalah
## The Earthquake

In the Name of God, the All-Merciful, the Most Merciful.

1. When the earth is shaken with her shaking
2. and the earth bringeth forth her burdens
3. and the human saith: What aileth her? —
4. that Day she shall tell her tidings,
5. for that thy Lord hath inspired her.
6. That Day, the people shall come forth in scattered groups to be shown their deeds.
7. So whoso doeth an atom's weight of good shall see it
8. and whoso doeth an atom's weight of evil shall see it.
