# Al-Ma'un
## Small Kindnesses

In the Name of God, the All-Merciful, the Most Merciful.

1. Hast thou seen him who denieth the Judgement?
2. That is he who repelleth the orphan
3. and urgeth not the feeding of the destitute.
4. So woe unto the praying ones
5. who are heedless of their prayer,
6. who make display
7. and withhold small kindnesses.
