# Al-Fil
## The Elephant

In the Name of God, the All-Merciful, the Most Merciful.

1. Hast thou not seen how thy Lord dealt with the people of the Elephant?
2. Did He not make their plot go astray?
3. And He sent against them birds in flocks,
4. pelting them with stones of baked clay,
5. and He made them like devoured husks.
